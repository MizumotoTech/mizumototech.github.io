## Entry offers

Entry engagements can usually be quoted as fixed-fee packages once scope,
deliverables, and access boundaries are clear. Written deliverables come
first; implementation support is scoped separately.

### Cloud Cost Hygiene Review

**Best for:** teams watching GCP bills rise without a clear plan.

One GCP project or billing account. Typically 1–2 weeks.

You receive a cost issue list, idle-resource report, prioritised savings
actions, and follow-up improvement recommendations.

### Architecture and Reliability Review

**Best for:** teams about to launch, scale, or prepare for audit review.

One workload or GCP project. Typically 2–3 weeks.

You receive a risk profile across architecture, environments, access,
deployments, and observability — with a prioritised remediation list.

### Runbook and Handover Pack

**Best for:** teams facing key-person risk or client handover.

One workload or service. Typically 2–4 weeks.

You receive structured runbooks covering common fault scenarios and
recovery steps, an operations overview, and a handover-readiness
checklist.

## How we work

- **Founder-led.** Every engagement is scoped and reviewed by a senior SRE
  with production GCP experience.
- **Written-first.** Findings and decisions are documented. Verbal
  conversations support the written record.
- **Async-friendly.** Built for cross-timezone teams. Meetings are
  optional.
- **Reviewable outputs.** Version-controlled, diffable deliverables — not
  slide decks.
- **Scope before implementation.** Implementation support is available only
  after review is complete, access boundaries are clear, and sensitive work
  remains under senior review.
