Delivery Model

Japan-side oversight first. Scope, access, and risk boundaries before execution.

Japan-side technical oversight

High-sensitivity or high-accountability work requires Japan-side senior engineering involvement, especially where production access, sensitive operational context, or client-specific security requirements are involved.

Scoped implementation support

Delivery starts from a defined baseline, then turns agreed changes into reviewable Terraform, CI/CD, documentation, or handover outputs.

Future blended delivery

Selected lower-sensitivity implementation support may be scoped separately in future engagements, depending on delivery boundaries and risk profile.

Operating Standards

The work is designed to stay maintainable, reviewable, and transferable.

  • 01 GCP-first in Phase 1

    Initial delivery commitments stay focused on GCP rather than overpromising multi-cloud implementation.

  • 02 Risk profile first

    Delivery mode follows sensitivity, access level, and client requirements.

  • 03 Reviewable outputs

    Infrastructure changes, deployment flow, documentation, and handover assets should be easy to inspect.

  • 04 Public-safe communication

    Public material avoids non-public operational, customer, and sensitive commercial details.
