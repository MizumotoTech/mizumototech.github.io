Delivery Model

Senior judgment first. Scoped implementation second.

Mizumoto Tech is built around Japan-side senior engineering oversight. Architecture decisions, production-impacting reviews, access boundaries, and final delivery validation are handled through Japan-side senior engineering oversight.

Security-sensitive work requires direct Japan-side handling, strict access boundaries, or separate client approval. Documentation, templates, checklists, demo repositories, and reviewable implementation drafts may be handled as scoped support work when inputs, outputs, and review conditions are clearly defined.

Operating Principles

Clear boundaries before delivery work begins.

  • 01 Risk profile first

    We clarify access level, production impact, security sensitivity, and review requirements before implementation.

  • 02 Reviewable outputs

    Reports, pull requests, runbooks, checklists, and diagrams are designed to be reviewed and handed over.

  • 03 Production-safe delivery

    High-sensitivity work remains under senior engineering review and direct delivery control. Final review and delivery responsibility stay with the senior engineering layer.

  • 04 Public-safe communication

    We avoid unnecessary exposure of customer secrets, logs, credentials, and production-sensitive details.
